## Bottom Line

The article is a standard geopolitical report on China's diplomatic and trade relations with the Andean Community. While it mentions AI and EV industries, it does so only to explain the strategic need for raw materials (lithium, copper) and the geopolitical competition between China and the US. It does not engage with the theory's core questions regarding the mode of production, the distinction between rent and profit, or the specific constitutive role of the state in building the internal economic logic of tech firms. It is a story about trade and resource security, not political economy theory.
